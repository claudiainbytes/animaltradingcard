Red-Footed Tortoise

The red-footed tortoise is a species of tortoises from northern South America.

  • Scientific Name: Chelonoidis carbonaria
  • Average Length: 12–14 in (30–35 cm)
  • Average Lifespan: 50 years
  • Habitat: Dry forest areas, grasslands and the savanna

They have dark-colored, loaf-shaped carapaces (back shell) with a lighter patch in the middle of each scute (scales on the shell), and dark limbs with brightly colored scales that range from pale yellow to dark red. Recognized differences are seen between red-footed tortoises from different regions.
